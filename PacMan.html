Developed and released, in Japan, by Namco in 1980, Pac-Man quickly became immensely popular because of his arcade game Pac-Man.

Pac-Man became so pupolar that many other companies went to designing games similar to Pac-Man in order to get in on the Pac-Man craze. This led to the development of Ms. Pac-Man, Jr. Pac-Man, Baby Pac-Man, Pac-Man Plus, and many more.

Most recently Pac-Man has been the protagonist of the Pac-Man and the Ghostly Adventures games, the Pac-Man Championship Edition games, Pac-Man Party, Pac-Man 256, and even guest starred in Super Smash Bros 4 Wii U.
